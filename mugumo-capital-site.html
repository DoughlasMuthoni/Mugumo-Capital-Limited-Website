Who We Are

A Kenyan Capital Raising Partner Built for East Africa.

Mugumo Capital Partners Limited is a Nairobi-headquartered capital advisory firm. We arrange, structure, and syndicate capital for project sponsors, developers, and financial institutions operating across Kenya and the East African Community.

Our focus is deliberate. We work where we have deep conviction, strong relationships, and a track record — affordable housing, renewable energy, Public Private Partnerships, and the microfinance sector. We do not pretend to serve every market. We serve East Africa, at the level of scale our partners need and with the discipline investors demand.

Our Services

Four Focused Service Lines. One Regional Mandate.

We specialise where capital gaps are deepest and where East African infrastructure and inclusive finance need patient, well-structured money. Every engagement is scoped, priced, and delivered for the East African context.

01 / Project Finance

Fundraising for Affordable Housing

We arrange debt, equity, and blended finance for affordable housing developers — from land acquisition and infrastructure servicing through to construction finance and end-user mortgage takeout.

  • Development and construction finance
  • Housing fund structuring and syndication
  • Blended finance with DFIs and concessional capital
  • Partnership with pension funds and housing finance institutions
02 / Project Finance

Fundraising for Renewable Energy

We raise project capital for utility-scale and distributed renewable energy across East Africa. Solar, hydro, wind, and geothermal — from greenfield development through to refinancing of operational assets.

  • Solar: utility-scale, commercial and industrial, and productive-use
  • Hydro: small, mini, and run-of-river projects
  • Wind: onshore wind project development
  • Geothermal: drilling, steamfield, and power generation finance
03 / Public Private Partnerships

PPP Project Advisory & Capital Raising

We advise contracting authorities and private sponsors on Public Private Partnership projects in Kenya. From project identification through feasibility, procurement, financial structuring, and commercial close — aligned with the National Treasury PPP Directorate framework.

  • PPP project origination, ranking, and prioritisation support
  • Project appraisal, feasibility studies, and bankability assessment
  • Transaction structuring, procurement, and tender evaluation advisory
  • Contract negotiation, financial close, and contract management
  • Private sector bid advisory for PPP tenders and concessions
04 / Institutional

Capital Raising for Microfinance

We raise senior debt, subordinated debt, and Tier 2 capital for microfinance banks, credit-only microfinance institutions, and inclusive finance platforms scaling across Kenya and East Africa.

  • On-lending debt facilities from DFIs and impact investors
  • Local currency funding and hedged foreign currency lines
  • Tier 2 subordinated capital and hybrid instruments
  • Receivables and portfolio-backed financing structures

Public Private Partnerships

Aligned with Kenya's PPP Legal & Institutional Framework.

Mugumo Capital Partners advises on Public Private Partnership projects within the framework established by Kenya's Public Private Partnership Act and administered by the PPP Directorate at the National Treasury — the technical arm of the PPP Committee responsible for originating, guiding, and coordinating PPP projects across contracting authorities.

We work across the full project cycle — from identification, appraisal, and feasibility through to procurement, tender evaluation, contract negotiation, financial close, and long-term contract management. We support contracting authorities on the public side and private sponsors and investors on the bid side, with strict attention to fiscal risk, bankability, and value for money.
